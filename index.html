Privacy Policy for No Ad Apps

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy applies to all apps published by No Ad Apps, including No Ad Hangman and No Ad Currency Converter.

Information We Collect

No Ad Apps does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data. We do not know who you are, where you are, or how you use our apps.

Data Storage

All app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Exchange Rate Data (Currency Converter)

The Currency Converter app fetches publicly available exchange rate data to display current rates. No personal information is sent as part of this request.

Third Parties

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services of any kind.

In-App Purchases

Where applicable, purchase information is handled entirely by Apple's App Store and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
